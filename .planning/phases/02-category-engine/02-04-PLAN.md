<objective>
Build AttributeDefinitionService (the schema registry that makes products vertical-agnostic) and FilterSchemaService (which attributes become storefront facets). Both are db-only services (no cache — infrequent hot-path reads in Phase 2). The attribute service enforces the options business rule (D-05/D-07); the filter service enforces that only filterable attributes can become facets.

Purpose: These two services are the CAT-03 and CAT-04 deliverables and the stable contract Phase 3 reads to validate product attributes and generate OpenSearch facet config.
Output: Two services + barrels + unit tests.
</objective>
